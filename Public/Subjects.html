Detailed Subject Breakdown

Explore the diverse range of courses offered across our three main academic streams for High School students (Grades 9-12).

Science Stream

Ideal for students pursuing careers in engineering, medicine, and research.

Core Subjects:
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology / Computer Science (Choice)
  • Mathematics / Advanced Mathematics (Choice)
Electives:
  • Robotics & AI
  • Web Development

Commerce Stream

Prepares students for finance, business, accounting, and management careers.

Core Subjects:
  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • Statistics / Marketing (Choice)
Electives:
  • Advanced Excel & Data Analysis
  • Financial Management

Humanities Stream

Focuses on law, journalism, social sciences, and creative fields.

Core Subjects:
  • History & Geography
  • Political Science & Civics
  • Psychology & Sociology
  • English Literature (AP Level)
Electives:
  • Fine Arts & Design
  • Spanish/French Language
**Note:** All streams require core instruction in English Language, Physical Education, and Ethics/Moral Science.
