Principal's Message

From Sr. Rosey Yacoob

"It is with immense pride that I welcome you to St. Mary’s School, a place where tradition meets innovation. For over a century, we have dedicated ourselves to fostering a learning environment where curiosity thrives and potential is unlocked. Our mission is to educate the whole person—mind, body, and spirit—preparing them not just for examinations, but for a life of purpose, leadership, and ethical contribution to the world."

Sr. Rosey Yacoob

Principal, St. Mary's School
